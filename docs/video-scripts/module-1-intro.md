# Module 1 Intro — Your AI Toolkit

- **Instructor:** Wes
- **Target runtime:** ~75 seconds
- **Audience:** Cashman employees, brand new to AI
- **Goal:** Welcome the learner, demystify AI, and introduce the three tools they'll use throughout the course.

## Script

Hey, I'm Wes, in case you haven't noticed, I'm an AI Avatar -- welcome to Cashman's AI training. We wanted to show you what's possible with AI. 

If the words "artificial intelligence" feel intimidating, take a breath. You've actually been using AI for years. Spell check is AI. Spam filters are AI. The autocomplete on your phone is AI.

What changed a couple of years ago is that AI became *conversational*. Instead of clicking buttons or typing keywords, you can just describe what you need -- in plain English -- and the AI responds.

In this first module, I'm going to introduce the three AI tools we use at Cashman. The **Cashman AI Portal**, which runs on our own servers, so your data stays inside the company. **Microsoft Copilot**, which lives inside Outlook, Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. And **ChatGPT**, the well-known public AI chatbot -- great for general questions, learning, and brainstorming when no company data is involved.

You'll also learn the **ground rules** -- the simple, common-sense guardrails that keep us, our clients, and our projects safe. Things like: if you wouldn't pin it on a public bulletin board in the lobby, don't paste it into a public AI tool.

By the end of this module, you'll know which tool to reach for, when to use it, and what *not* to do. Let's get started.
