- **Goal:** Position email as the highest-ROI starting point for AI, preview the three lessons, and set up the first real "try it now" exercise.

## Script

Welcome back. I'm Wes, and this is Module 2.

If you only ever use AI for one thing at work, make it **email**. Here's why: most of us spend an hour or more every day just reading, writing, or untangling email threads. AI can take a serious bite out of that.

In this module, we're going to do three very practical things.

First, we're going to take a poorly-written email -- the kind we've all sent on a bad day -- and watch AI turn it into something professional and clear in about ten seconds.

Second, we'll take a tangled, 47-reply project thread and ask AI to pull out what actually matters: the current status, the decisions, and the action items.

And third -- this one's the most useful -- we'll write a difficult response. The kind of email you stare at for twenty minutes trying to find the right tone. AI is genuinely great at helping you sound firm, professional, and calm, even when you don't feel that way.

I'm also going to show you a security risk called **prompt injection**. It's a fancy term for something simple: an outsider hiding instructions inside an email to try to trick the AI. You'll learn to spot it.

By the end of Module 2, you'll have a playbook you can use on every email, every day. Let's dig in.
